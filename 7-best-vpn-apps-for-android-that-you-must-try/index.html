7 Best VPN Apps For Android That You Must Try

  • May 23, 2022May 31, 2022

Virtual Private Networks (VPNs) are apps that allow you to hide your IP address, access geo-restricted material, and more. VPNs are secure and stringent for users’ privacy and security because of their no-logging policy and IP address masking architecture.

You may easily view several Netflix libraries and other platforms with high-quality VPNs. It lets you evade any geo-restrictions imposed by governments and other institutions and rigorous supplemental surveillance. The question is, which VPN program for Android is the best? If you’re curious about the solution to this question, keep reading.

ExpressVPN

One of our favorite VPN apps for Android is ExpressVPN. It has over 3,000 servers scattered over 94 countries and 160+ locations, giving it a significantly wider network coverage to experiment with. Unlike many other VPNs, ExpressVPN is not a member of any alliance, so your data is protected without being shared for monitoring. It has dedicated Netflix content-streaming servers and many other streaming sites and torrenting, so you’re covered.

ExpressVPN’s shifting IP address decreases the chances of a hacker attempting to track you. In addition, because there is minimal logging, nothing you visit or do online is saved on the servers. A kill switch, 24/7 customer assistance, and five simultaneous connections are also included.

Features:

  • More than 3,000 servers in 160 locations across 94 countries
  • Exclusive streaming servers for Netflix, Hulu, and Prime Video
  • 24/7 customer service and a wealth of tutorials
  • AES 256-bit encryption uses in the programme.
  • Maximum of five connections

Price:

$12.95 for one month, $9.99 for six months, and $6.67 for a year (1 year)

NordVPN

NordVPN is a top-rated VPN app for Android smartphones on the Google Play Store. It is not a member of any monitoring alliance, which means it is safe from surveillance. The app uses Double VPN technology, which encrypts data and routes it through two different VPN servers. NordVPN presently has over 5,200 servers in 62 other locations. Within the NordVPN app, you may select server locations on a map.

The upgraded CyberSec malware protection, the NordLynx protocol, and split tunneling are only a few features. The service supports six simultaneous connections with dedicated IP addresses. Also included is an automated kill switch. Additionally, NordVPN has a no-logs policy, so you can be confident that no data is being logged while you’re using it.

Features:

  • More than 5,200 servers in 62 places
  • 24/7 customer support
  • Multiple encryption steps
  • Six maximum devices
  • 30 days to return it

Price:

$11.99 for one month, $4.99 for one year, and $3.29 per month (2 years)

Surfshark

SurfShark is one of the most potent VPN apps for Android. It has more than 3,200 servers in 65 service locations. In addition, you can use its multihop connection, which hides your IP address and what you do while on a VPN. As a result, it’s easy to connect as many devices as you want to a single SurfShark connection.

SurfShark is well-made, putting it at the top of the list for both new and experienced VPN users. You can also add things to it, like antivirus software. But there are some problems, like the fact that there are no dedicated streaming servers and no phone support. Also, when you choose a server, the app doesn’t show you how busy that server is, which could cause buffering if there are too many people on it.

Features:

More than 3,200 servers in more than 65 server locations
Split tunneling and multihop connections
Very well-thought-out interface
Connecting as many devices as you want
Price:

$12.95 per month, $3.99 per month for one year, and $2.33 per month for two years, with a 30-day money-back guarantee.

CyberGhost

If you don’t like how free VPNs often have bugs, you should switch to CyberGhost. It’s a paid VPN service that gives you access to many features. It has more than 7,000 servers with fast connections, and all the data moving through it is encrypted with AES-256 encryption. In addition, content Blocker keeps you from seeing apps and websites that could let malware in.

CyberGhost’s internet speeds are breakneck. WireGuard has a rate of 1Gbps in the UK, which makes it one of the fastest VPNs. You can sign up for this VPN and enjoy it because it doesn’t keep logs, has servers in 90 countries, and has many premium features.

Features:

  • AES-256 encryption Auto-kill switch for up to seven devices
  • Over 7,000 servers in over 90 countries
  • The fast Internet connection

Prices:

Content Blocker costs $12.99 per month, $7.99 per month for six months, and $2.75 per month (18 months)

TunnelBear

TunnelBear isn’t your average person. It stores more than 10 million downloads on Google Play Store alone, which is a lot. It uses AES-256 encryption to keep all of your information safe. Its minimal logging policy gives you peace of mind that TunnelBear won’t use your information to show you ads. It has a vital server infrastructure with more than 1800 servers in 26 countries. If you like to stream content, YouTube, DAZN, and Netflix have servers just for that.

Even though there are some problems with the app, such as no live chat and sometimes shaky connections, you can’t choose the server’s region. TunnelBear, on the other hand, has a lot to offer as well. First, there’s VigilantBear, which has a kill switch if you ever lose your VPN connection while connecting to the internet. Its GhostBear servers use unique algorithms to route data so that it looks like it came from a service that is not a VPN.

Features:

  • There are over 1,800 servers in 26 countries.
  • VigilantBear and GhostBear The free plan has a monthly limit.
  • Minimal logging policy
  • It is encrypted with AES-256.

Price:

Five VPN connections with a monthly cap of 500 MB cost $0, TunnelBear Unlimited with five VPN connections costs $3.33/mo, and TunnelBear Teams costs $17.25/mo.

Private Internet Access

Private Internet Access, or PIA, is another high-quality VPN for Android devices that gives you security, privacy, and an easy-to-use VPN with more than 12,000 servers. In addition, the app has advanced VPN tools like a kill switch, WireGuard protocols, split tunneling, and tracker blocking, and that’s just the icing on the cake.

If you want to try out the app before paying for it, you can do so for seven days for free. There are some problems, though. For example, it’s part of the Five Eyes alliance, and the server has gone down a few times. It has reasonable prices, but you have to stay with it for more extended periods.

Features:

  • Standards for solid encryption
  • Ten connections simultaneously
  • Support available 24/7
  • Trackers, ads, and DNS malware can be blocked by splitting the tunnel.

Price: $11.95 per month, $3.33 per month for a year, and $2.03 per month (3 years)

VyprVPN

VyprVPN is an Android VPN app that is just getting started. It has a network of more than 700 servers in more than 70 different countries. It has fast servers that make it easy to browse and stream content. VyprVPN is also a fun place to play games.

The app doesn’t keep any logs, even when torrenting, so it doesn’t store any information. Instead, the Chameleon Protocol in VyprVPN hides your activities to protect your identity and keep you anonymous on the internet. It can hook onto up to 200k IP addresses. There is no limit on how much data you can use, and VyprVPN lets you watch the match as much as you want.

Features:

  • Torrenting-friendly
  • No logs policy
  • 700 services, over 70 server locations around the world, and over 70 countries
  • A kill switch for the Chameleon Protocol

Price:

$15 per month or $8.33 per month for a year.

Conclusion

It’s essential to choose the right VPN apps for Android based on how you plan to use them. For example, it could be used to switch between Netflix servers (if it detects VPN or otherwise). It could be, among other things, to hide your actual IP address. VPNs are the best way to get around geo-restrictions and unlock features that might only be available in one country. For example, WhatsApp VoIP doesn’t work in the UAE unless you have a VPN, so you have to have one.
