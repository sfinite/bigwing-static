10 Best Free Video Editing Software Programs for 2022

  • May 24, 2022May 31, 2022

Wyzowl’s 2022 State of Video Marketing found in a survey that 86% of businesses still use video as a marketing tool. You’ll need good video editing software to show off your products from every angle by making professional promos for Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, and other places where brand videos are shared. Many free video editing programs are easy to use and help you stand out from the crowd.

Well-made product videos are a more exciting form of content marketing than just photos. They can make customers more likely to buy.

We’ve made a list of the best free video editing software for 2022 and some paid versions that aren’t too expensive. Of course, every marketer has different needs, so we’ve put together a list of some of the best parts of each tool.

What is Video Editing Software?

Video editing software is a collection of tools that help content creators edit, change, make, or otherwise work with a video or movie file. With video editing software that you can change, you can cut and rearrange video to make it flow better, take out parts, and add effects to make the video better. No matter how big or small, any company can use a good video editor to create compelling content.
Best free video editing software for desktop
Businesses that want to make promotional videos have a lot of desktop-based editing software to choose from. Most free mobile video editing apps don’t have as many features as free desktop video editing software. This is the most significant benefit of free desktop video editing software.

If you want to edit your promotional videos on a budget, check out our list of free video editor software that you can use to edit long-form content for YouTube, your eCommerce site, and several other platforms:

Lightworks

The best free video editing software all around

Lightworks has an easy-to-use interface that makes it very easy to get started. Lightworks is infact one of the more accessible and easy-to-learn Linux video editing programs on this list. It is software for editing videos with a set of detailed video tutorials.

Lightworks comes with a set of original stock videos and music clips that can be used in any video you edit on the program. This lets you give any marketing video a polished look.

Features:

  • Simple trim functions of high quality for fast and accurate video editing
  • Speed optimization lets you keep editing while importing new material.
  • Multicam editing lets you sync clips from different cameras.
  • Real-time sharing of team projects with complete control over who can make changes.
  • Videos are easy to share because they can be sent quickly to YouTube, Vimeo, Facebook, and Instagram.
  • A lot of different video formats can be played.

VideoPad

Best free software for beginners to learn how to edit videos

VideoPad is a great free choice for anyone who wants to make high-quality videos without spending a dime on software to edit them. You can try out different video styles and compositions with VideoPad’s simple and easy-to-use features, such as 3D video editing and a library of sound effects.

You can add special effects, overlays, texts, and transitions to your videos. You can also change the colors and add sound effects. Then you can quickly and easily send all of your finished videos to YouTube, Facebook, Google Drive, and other mobile devices.

The only problem is that it might be too easy for people who are good at making videos. Also, it doesn’t have as many advanced features as some of the other free video editing software on this list. Still, the ability to add light effects, fine-tune colors, and overlay text and images is enough to make it worth the free download.

Features:

  • One built-in screen lets you see previews of selected clips, and the other lets you see previews of the whole sequence.
  • Pre-made transitions that can be changed to look polished and professional
  • Exporting presets makes it easy to create video files optimized for specific platforms, like Facebook and YouTube, and post them straight from the software.
  • Tools for sharing and publishing on social media that let you share videos online right away
  • An massive collection of transitions and visual effects can be added to videos to make them unique.
  • Video optimization tools, such as stabilization

HitFilm Express

A simple, free video editor with professional visual effects and tutorials

HitFilm Express may seem complicated if you don’t have much experience editing videos. It’s used to make Hollywood-style movies, after all. But it’s worth the learning curve because it’s one of the best free all-in-one video editors. HitFilm does ask you to share a status update on social media when you download the software, but that’s not a big deal.

HitFilm has animation tools that let you make and edit transitions between audio and video without having to make composite shots. This saves alot of time when editing videos.

Features:

  • With tools like motion tracking, color pickers, and cropping, you can edit videos like a pro.
  • Composite shot enhancements let you move two different shots together to create strange video effects.
  • An automatic stabilizer that makes shaky video look steady
  • Make as many video and audio tracks on a timeline as possible.
  • An innovative trimmer that lets you change the length of your clips after you add them to the timeline
  • Ability to copy a set of properties from one clip and use them on another clip or the whole project.
  • An audio mixer lets you fine-tune sounds so that they sound professional.
  • There are different transitions, like push, zoom, and dissolve.

DaVinci Resolve

The best free video editor for experts

DaVinci Resolve is the video editing software for many professional editors of movies, TV shows, and commercials. It combines professional 8K editing, color correction, visual effects, and audio post-production into a single production tool.

DaVinci lets you edit both offline and online, and its high-performance playback engine saves time when editing and trimming, even for processor-intensive file formats like H.264 and RAW. Some users may find that DaVinci Resolve has too many features. Still, the free version is excellent for more advanced projects.

Features:

  • Two timelines make it easy to move around, cut, and trim.
  • A trim interface that lets you edit down to the smallest detail.
  • Audio and video clips are synced up automatically by intelligent editing software.
  • Built-in tools for picture-in-picture effects, retiming, stabilizing, dynamic zooming, text, and audio
  • Face detection in your clips that creates bins automatically based on the people in each shot
  • High-quality motion estimation and frame rate conversions to speed up or slow down clips quickly and realistically.

VSDC Free Video Editor

Only Windows users can use the best free video editing software.

The desktop video editing software from VSDC can be used for free or for a fee. Even though the free version has fewer features, it still has all VSDC’s video editing tools, like a mask tool and chroma key for green-screen effects.

If you can afford the full version, the tools grow to include video stabilization, strong post-production effects, slideshow/presentation features, 3D diagram templates, and direct publishing to social media.

Features:

  • Colour-correction tools to make your photos look more professional
  • A complete set of effects for making changes
  • Filters for photos that are easy to use, like those on Instagram
  • Mask tools let you hide, blur, or bring out certain elements.
  • Social media links for sharing videos on Facebook, YouTube, Instagram, Twitter, and Vimeo.
  • HEVC/H.265 (an advanced video-compression standard) keeps the highest quality at the smallest file size—essential for processing 4K and HD files.

OpenShot

The most accessible free software for intermediate video editors to use

OpenShot is a great video editing program for beginners because it is easy to use. Even though more complex tools may have more editing features, OpenShot’s simple design, and easy-to-use interface make it perfect for simple editing.

OpenShot is a good choice for small businesses because it is easy to use and is best for resizing, scaling, trimming, snapping, and rotating clips. It also lets you scroll through credits, step-through frames, map time, edit audio, and see a preview in real-time.

Features:

  • Watermarks, background videos, and audio tracks can be put on as many layers as possible.
  • Title-card templates that can be changed
  • The drag-and-drop files make adding video, audio, or image files easy.
  • Digital video effects, such as brightness, gamma, hue, greyscale, and chroma key compositing for green-screen effects
  • Keyframe animation is a powerful way to make animated videos.

Shotcut

Best open-source movie maker for audiophiles

Shotcut is a free, cross-platform, open-source video editor with a massive library of how-to videos. It also works with many formats, like FFmpeg, 4K, ProRes, and DNxHD. However, a word of caution: Shotcut was initially made for Linux, so some people may find it strange. Even so, it’s still a perfect editor.

Shotcut also has a lot of options for editing audio, which makes it perfect for people who love music. With its many tools for fine-tuning audio, users can change the bass and treble, create bandpass filters, change the gain, add dynamic compression, and do more.

Features:

  • The leading multimedia framework, FFmpeg, supports the newest audio and video formats.
  • Native timeline editing lets users import video files directly into their project file instead of a media library.
  • Multi-format timeline for mixing and matching different resolutions and frame rates within a project.
  • Balance, bass, treble, bandpass filters, compressor, and more are among the advanced audio filters used.

Blender

The best professional software for editing videos with a 3D creation suite

Blender is a free, open-source 3D creation suite that can be used to make videos. It has tools to help you create 3D models and render animations. Hundreds of developers from all over the world worked together to make the software. As a result, Blender’s software has many tools because it has a large community of users.

Blender is made to be a 3D animation suite. Still, it also comes with a video editor that most people should be able to use. Blender is a flexible video editor that both beginners and experts can use. It can do more complex edits like video masking and is an excellent choice for more advanced projects that may involve 3D animation.

Features:

  • Path-tracing engine for making animated objects look real
  • Advanced rendering methods to save and export your project faster
  • The software for making video games gives you the option to code game logic into it.
  • Advanced tools for animating characters with realistic movement.
  • an extensive collection of visual effects and short films.

Movie Maker 10

The best free software for Windows 10 to edit videos

Movie Maker 10 is an easy-to-use video editing program for Windows that lets you get back to basics. It’s great for editing short video clips quickly when you need them right away.

Movie Maker 10 has a free version that includes all of its tools for editing videos and pictures and the ability to add captions with fonts and colors that can be changed.

Features:

  • Automatic preview for changes and effects
  • Auto-movie options that let you cut your video clips in ways that suit you
  • You can use the animation tab to add music, titles, and credits quickly.

iMovie

Best free software for making videos for YouTube

iMovie is a popular program that comes with Mac OS. Like many other Apple apps, iMovie is easy to use and lets you move files from your drive to your workstation with a simple drag-and-drop interface. It’s a simple way to make powerful videos to help you market on YouTube.

IMovie saves files in Apple’s cloud is a big plus. This means you can work on your project on an iPhone, iPad, or Mac. Also, iMovie constantly gets new features and improvements because it is continuously updated.

Features:

  • With 4K resolution, users can make movies that look like they belong in a theatre.
  • You can work on the same project on your iPad, iPhone, and Mac with cloud storage.
  • Dozens of professional-looking title animations were made in a studio.
  • Styled, modern video filters made for certain types of movies, like Westerns and sci-fi.
  • With built-in music, sound effects, and voiceover recording, you can always edit the sound and the picture simultaneously.
  • Speed controls let you speed up action scenes and slow down sports highlights.

Conclusion

Now, so you know your options, you can try out a few to find the best free video editing software. Even though each choice has pros and cons, you should now know which free video-editing program will work best for your needs and the results you want.

Videos consistently get more engagement on average than pictures or text posts on all social media sites. Professionally made marketing videos can help you tell a story about your products, beat YouTube’s algorithm, grab your audience’s attention, give your brand a personality, and get the most out of your paid social ads.

Raise the bar on your content marketing. With so many options for free video editing software, the marketing videos for your brand can be as creative as you want them to be. Also, software for editing videos helps build your brand’s image with visuals that can be viewed on the go.
